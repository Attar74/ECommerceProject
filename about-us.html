Overview

The idea of the project is a website called “Swap Free”, it’s a social website that based on users who want or need to swap or exchange products online, upload a photo and publish the product for swap free with other user’s product. It also helps users to discover new products for swap/exchange.

 

 

Goals

The users open the website, login with a previously created account, if they don’t have an account they can create a new account, then the website home page is opened, they can use the website without an account but as anonymous or guest to discover products, but when the users start to swap/exchange they must login.

In the home the user get feeds related to his interests:

      • Products.
      • Music.
      • Movies.
      • Games.

 

 

Website swap service price

User can use the website for FREE, but may pay for paid service like featured products, and increase product spread throw notifications to similar users.

 

 

Principles of the website social design

The website satisfies the user needs and the principles of social design through:

      • Motivation.
      • Identity.
      • Control.
      • Independence.
      • Privacy.
      • Authority.
      • Community.

 

 

Website design with social intelligence

The most important point in the website design with social intelligence is that we work on:

      • Evaluate.
      • Learn.

Till delivering “Sustainable Competitive Advantage”.

 

 

Power points in the website

User-based:

The website is based on users, connection between them, to make the content easy to get and to get what they want quickly with a click.

 

Smart Search:

One of the most awesome points in the website which help users to get what they want without asking, only they can search with what they want and the smart search analyses what are written and also with tags get the perfect results of products.

Smart Search working with the analysis of the users interests, searches, products, and communications to help them get the perfect results.

 

Simple Design:

The website design is very simple and based on “Less is more” and “Keep it super simple”, which make the users feel great.

 

Security:

The website users data is secured with end to end encryption . Which users can put a pin code to open it.

 

Privacy:

The user can control in whole of the website parts, he can choose who can swap with him and see his products.

 

Synchronization:

All of the users’ data, settings and more are synchronized to the cloud to be saved.
